Duh Trends Web-Based Business Operations Management System

Web Application Idea

Description: The Duh Trends Web-Based Sales and Inventory Management System is designed to streamline the operations of Duh Trends by automating the sales recording and inventory tracking process for vendors. Currently, vendors rely on manual methods to track their stocks, while store staff are responsible for recording sales based on item codes. This traditional approach can lead to errors, inefficiencies, and difficulties in managing inventory effectively.

Purpose: The purpose of this project is to provide a centralized, web-based platform where vendors can monitor their sales and manage their inventory more efficiently. Instead of relying on separate spreadsheets or manual stock counting, vendors will be able to access real-time sales reports and adjust their supply accordingly.

Features:

  • Renter Dashboard
  • Staff Dashboard
  • Management Dashboard
  • Inventory Management
  • Sales Management
  • User Management
  • Editing Items, Stocks, & etc.

Technologies Used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js with Express.j, MySQL, Railway (for Cloud Database).
